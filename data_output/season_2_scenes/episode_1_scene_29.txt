[In the playground.]

Ms. De Brn: Dig deep, something you hate, something you despise. No holding back! Come on! Get it out! 

(She throws a ball at Erin, first of the class in the queue.)

Erin: (hitting the ball) Injustice!

Ms. De Brn: Yes! (throwing ball at Clare)

Clare: (hitting the ball) Prejudice!

Ms. De Brn: Good! This is good!
